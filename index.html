Jurgen Klopp explains why talks over Mohamed Salah contract are taking time

Jurgen Klopp believes there is "nothing to worry about" regarding the continued impasse between Liverpool and Mohamed Salah over a new contract. Salah, who is now in the final 18 months of his current deal, has once again addressed the topic of his Anfield future in an interview with GQ magazine. The forward, currently starring with Egypt in the Africa Cup of Nations, insisted he was "not asking for crazy stuff" and reiterated the matter was in the hands of Liverpool and owners Fenway Sports Group. And while Klopp admits negotiations between the relevant parties are taking time, he is confident a resolution can be reached.
